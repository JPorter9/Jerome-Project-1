About us

Founded in 2020, during covid/lockdown times Head coach Elliot Defreitas started a successful one to one coaching business. From there he carried on to form a team with his brother Essex Swish is an Essex based basketball club delivering high quality basketball coaching across the county. We provide teams for U12's, U16 boys as well as two men's teams.
