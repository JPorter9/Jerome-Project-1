About us

Founded in 2020, during covid/lockdown, Head coach Elliot Defreitas started a successful one to one coaching business. From there he carried on to form a team with his brother Essex Swish is an Essex based basketball club delivering high quality basketball coaching across the county. We provide teams for U12's, U16 boys as well as two men's teams.

Player of the Game

Last weeks Player of the Game was Ish Gwegweni, with a stunning performance to lead Swish Orange to a great victory against Erkenwald. His leadership on defence prevented the opposition from running their plays successfully. He racked up 5 steals in the second quarter alone! Ish ended up with a total of 10 points, 6 assists, 7 steals and 3 rebounds.
